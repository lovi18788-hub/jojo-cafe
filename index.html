Our Story

Born from a Love of Coffee

Jojo Café began as a simple dream — a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee would greet every soul that walked through the door. Nestled in the heart of Sahnewal, Ludhiana, we have been the neighbourhood's favourite corner since 2018.

"We don't just serve coffee. We serve moments worth remembering — mornings that matter and evenings that linger."

Every bean is sourced with care. Every recipe is crafted with love. Every table is a place where stories begin. That is the Jojo promise.

Friday evenings come alive with live acoustic performances. Sundays are for slow mornings with all-day breakfast. Every day offers something to look forward to.
